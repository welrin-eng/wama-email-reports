Email Health
✅
Deliverability is excellent
All 10 campaigns landed at 99.9%+ delivery. No inbox placement issues. Sender reputation is in great shape.
✅
Open rates are strong
Average open rate across all campaigns was 63.5% - well above industry average. The engaged list strategy is working.
⚠
Click rates have room to improve
7 of 10 campaigns were below the 1% click target. Opens are strong - the opportunity is converting that interest into clicks.
✅
Flows driving 74% of email revenue
Welcome Series alone brought in $20,889 - 54% of all email revenue for the month. Automations are the backbone.
What to Watch in June
Click rates - testing stronger CTAs and simpler layouts
Opens are consistently 60%+, but most campaigns aren't converting that to clicks. In June we'll test higher CTA placement, fewer distractions in the email body, and stronger call-to-action copy to close this gap.
Wallet Balance segment - grow the audience
The Wallet Balance Reminder earned $0.34 per recipient - the highest RPR of the month. The list is only 898 people. Growing this segment and building a dedicated flow for reward earners is a low-effort, high-return opportunity.
Re-engagement flow - refresh underway
The Re-engagement 30D flow is underperforming against benchmarks. We'll update subject lines and copy to give lapsed subscribers a better reason to come back - with a soft offer to drive action.
